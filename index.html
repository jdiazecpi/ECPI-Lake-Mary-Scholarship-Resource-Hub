ECPI Scholarships

1 External · 6 Internal

ECPI University awards in excess of $14 million in scholarships each year. Applicants must have applied and been accepted for admissions to ECPI University and meet all entrance requirements.

External · Military

Active Duty Military Scholarship

For eligible active duty service members and reservists who are also eligible for Tuition Assistance (TA). Effectively reduces tuition charges to the TA level of $250 per credit hour. Fees are not covered.

Up to TA Level · $250/cr. hr.

Internal Institutional Scholarships — Administered Through Financial Assistance

Internal

ECPI Armed Services Scholarship for Active Duty Service Members and Spouses

Open to Active Duty, Reservists, National Guard, and their spouses pursuing undergraduate degrees. Award: $500.00 per semester / $1,000.00 per academic year. Available at all campus locations and online. Ask your admissions advisor for details.

Internal

Graduation Scholarship Fund

Auto-enrolled for all undergraduate Bachelor's, Associate's, or diploma students. Provides awards up to $1,000 applied first to reduce student loan debt in the final semester. To be eligible you must:

  • Regularly attend full-time and attend at least 85% of all class time in your first semester
  • Make Satisfactory Progress as defined in the university catalog
  • Complete three financial literacy modules on ECPI's financial literacy platform
  • Have financial need as determined by the FAFSA

The following four scholarships below are available only for eligible programs: Associate Degree in Nursing · BSN (Traditional Track) · Diagnostic Medical Sonography · Medical Radiography · Physical Therapist Assistant · Practical Nursing

How Else Can I Pay?

Additional Financing Options

Federal Grants

Federal grants, like the Pell Grant, provide need-based assistance that does not have to be repaid. Complete the FAFSA to determine your eligibility. Use ECPI University school code 010198.

Apply at StudentAid.gov →

Federal Student Loans

Low-interest government loans often without a credit check or cosigner. Repayment typically defers until six months after graduation with flexible repayment plans. Complete the FAFSA using school code 010198.

ECPI Financial Assistance

Review all of ECPI's borrowing options, including federal and private loans, and connect with a Financial Assistance advisor.

Explore Borrowing Options →

Private Loans & Smart Borrowing

Banks, credit unions, and private lenders offer education loans. Thoroughly review interest rates and repayment terms. Remember: you can borrow less than the amount offered — speak with your advisor to borrow only what you need.
